      <h3>item banc collects local prices of items that are basic human needs.
          The people that enter the prices are called Validators. 
          The Validators are rewarded with special tokens. 
          These same tokens can be used to see prices entered by other Validators.
          Item banc collects the prices from around the world and uses them together to calculate
          a new kind of Value base, similar to purchasing power parity, where the same basic set of 
          items are compared around the world to discover a relative value of a currency.
          This new Value base is the Item Banc Index.
          The tokens pay for getting item banc index information as well.
          There are about 100 or so items represented in the item banc.
          In our initial roll-out, we will only look at 14 items in five categories, as below.
          Welcome! If you would like to Validate for your country, enter your name below:
